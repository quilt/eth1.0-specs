.. function:: add(evm: ethereum.vm.Evm) -> None
   :noindexentry:

   Adds the top two elements of the stack together, and pushes the result back
   on the stack.

   :param evm: The current EVM frame.

   :raises StackUnderflowError: If `len(stack)` is less than `2`.
